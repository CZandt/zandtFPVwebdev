California Born, Seattle Raised About The Pilot

I am a Seattle native currently doing classes at BYU. Over COVID lockdowns I re-discovered FPV drones and my love for flying that I had forgotten years earlier. Now being employed I could finally get my own drones and quickly upgrade from a micro to a 4 inch and then to the 5 inch I fly to this day. I have fallen in love with being able to create unique and cool videos by pushing my editing abilities along with my flying skills.

See Conact Info I learned to edit videos from making short clips for instagram when I did competitive trampoline. These skills I learned from doing trampoline and editing videos have significantly helped me when flying drones. Doing acrobatic stunts with the drone and being able to know where I am at all times is just like doing complex skills on a trampoline.
