Saint Anthony, ID Sand Dune Sunsets

I had 0 idea about the existance of sand dunes in the state of Idaho. When we got to the location it was hard to believe what we were seeing, it was incredible and insanely gorgeous. This shoot was anything but easy though. The sand gets everywhere and will ground the drone if it comes in contact with it, becuase of this all the footage was from one pack which is why I like calling this shoot a one pack wonder.See the video!

Castle Rock, CO Mile High Highways

Shooting in the mountains outside of Denver CO was an incredible experiance. It was the most high speed car chasing I have done to date which was a fun challenge and makes for an amazing video. In addition this video shoot was right on the edge of a storm. Pretty quick after we packed everything back in the truck the rain and lightning started. You can notice the heavy rain in the background of a lot of the shots.

Snoqualmie, WA West Coast Cruising

This was a special video shoot because it is in my home state of Washington. This shoot was also the first weekend I had the 5inch drone. Before this I had only chased cars with a smaller 4inch without a camera. You can tell the video is a little rough on the edges but it is still one of my favorite shoots and shows the growth that I have undergone since starting to film FPV video.

Provo, UT Canyon Curves

Shooting up in the canyon during the fall was a beautiful video shoot. The colors mixed with the darker colors of the sky and the car make it look really cool. Utah valley is an area I drag on for being boring all the time but this is one of the most interesting videos. The tight canyon roads also posed an additional challenge which was super fun to work with and pushed me to get within inches of the car in some of the videos.

Provo, UT Three Wheel Wonder

Being able to shoot a Vanderhall was an incredible experiance. That car is truly one of a kind and is worth checking out even if you are remotely interested. Sadly due to current technological problems this project and others are still in the process of being edited.

Lindon, UT Model Plane Chase

A RC model plane is probably the hardest thing that I have chased with the drone yet. It is very small, very agile, and very hard to mimic the flight characteristics of a plane with just a quadcopter. Definitely pushed me to be a better pilot and taught me some great lessons.
